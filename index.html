About Us

ST STONE MEDICAL DEVICES PVT LTD...

St.Stone Medical Devices Private Limited was incorporated in 2012 and start it’s planning for one of the biggest project in North East India. Company has started it’s Official venture in 2014 by setup of Manufacturing Plant in North East India.

Although as a new born company, having vast vision and mission to achieve. granted the licence for manufacturing and Distribution of Medical Devices first time in North East India by CDSCO & State Drug Authority. We are into manufacturing & assembling of medical devices having vast range which falls under Interventional Cardiology Consumable, Nephrology, Anaesthesia and General Consumables.

We are Quality Certified ISO 13485:2012 and ISO 9001:2008 Accreditation Body., CE Certification (Comp.), WHO-GMP Certification(Comp.) we are proud member of Pharmaexcil, All India Medical Device Association.
